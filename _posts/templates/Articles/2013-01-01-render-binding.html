Render binding

The render binding is used internally in wipeout to render view models, and can also be used in application development

window.renderVm = wo.contentControl.extend(function renderVm() { this._super(); this.innerItem = new wo.contentControl(); this.innerItem.template("Hello wipeout!"); }); <renderVm> <template> <div data-bind="render: innerItem"></div> </template> </renderVm> <wo.view templateId="'render-binding-script-1'" />
